About

You might have come across OpenAI's DALL-E project. This is an artificial intelligence that can generate images from text descriptions. However, it disappoints in creating simple, abstract art. DALL-F creates art inspired by works such as Convergence and Composition II with Red, Blue and Yellow.

DALL-F is not only blazingly fast, platform-independent and completely free, but also requires hardly any resources. This is mainly due to the fact, that instead of neural networks, DALL-F relies on the power of JavaScript's Math.random function, which significantly outperforms any artificial intelligence.
In addition, DALL-F offers you many customisation options. You can even submit your art to be evaluated by art critics. All you have to do is fill in the form above. You will likely receive the result by email within a few weeks.

In a direct comparison, DALL-E generated the image below in 14.61s, while DALL-F took merely 1.42ms (about 10'000 times faster). Like it? DALL-F 2 is already in the works. It will add a lot of new features, such as curves, polygons and even freeform shapes.
